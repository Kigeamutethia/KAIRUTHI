Design a clean, modern, and professional multi-page website for **Kairuthi & Co. Advocates LLP**, a full-service law firm established in 2022. The website should feel trustworthy, authoritative, and refined.

---

# **Brand Identity & Styling**

### **Color Palette**

* Primary accent: **#95695e**
* Deep green (footer & headings): **#1e3c12**
* Button color: **#1d4a14** with white text
* Background: **#ffffff**, alternating sections with **#e9ede8**
* Header background: **#ffffff** with **#000000** text
* Body text: **#000000**

### **Typography**

* Headings: Elegant serif (Playfair Display or Merriweather)
* Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter or Source Sans 3)

### **Imagery Style**

Use high-quality, realistic professional imagery:

* Lawyers consulting clients
* Modern law offices and meeting rooms
* Legal documents, contracts, law books
* Courthouse architecture (subtle, modern)
* Muted tones, natural lighting, calm atmosphere
  Avoid dramatic courtroom visuals.

---

# **HEADER & NAVIGATION (IMPORTANT)**

Design a **horizontal header with content arranged from left to right**:

### **Left Side**

* Firm logo
* Firm name: **Kairuthi & Co. Advocates LLP**

### **Center Navigation Menu**

* Home
* About the Firm
* Legal Practice Areas
* Insights
* Contact

Menu should be evenly spaced, clean, and minimal.

### **Right Side**

* Primary CTA button: **“Contact Us”** or **“Book a Consultation”**

  * Background: **#1d4a14**
  * Text: **#ffffff**
  * Rounded corners
  * Hover effect: darker green

### **Header Styling**

* Background: **#ffffff**
* Text: **#000000**
* Sticky header on scroll
* Subtle bottom border or soft shadow for separation
* Modern, minimal, professional spacing
